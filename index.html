üí° Essential Travel Tips

Prepare for your journey with our expert advice, categorized for easy reference:

Before You Go

  • **Check Expiration Dates:** Ensure your passport is valid for at least six months *after* your return date.
  • **Notify Your Bank:** Inform your credit card company of your travel dates to prevent fraud alerts or freezing of your accounts.
  • **Digital Copies:** Keep digital copies of your passport, visa, and insurance stored securely online.

On the Ground

  1. **Stay Hydrated:** Carry a reusable water bottle, especially in hot climates.
  2. **Use Local Transport:** This is often the most authentic and cost-effective way to explore a city.
  3. **Respect Local Customs:** Research and follow local etiquette to ensure a positive experience for everyone.
